1. Executive Dashboard (Home)
Purpose: Serves as the central command center, providing a "snapshot" of the farm's current operational status and alerting the owner to critical issues.

Features:
    - Farm Status Overview: Real-time card counters for Total Roosters, Total Sales (Monthly/Yearly), and Low Stock Alerts.
    - Navigation Hub: Quick access to History, Information, Ratings, and Inventory.
    - Gallery Preview: Display of featured roosters currently available for sale.

2. Rooster Inventory Management
Purpose: Manages the lifecycle and data of individual roosters from arrival to sale, replacing paper-based records.

Features:
    - Rooster Profiling: Create/Edit profiles with fields for Breed, Age, and Weight.
    - Digital Catalog: Upload images for the sales display.
    - Status Tracking: Tag roosters as "Available," "Sold," "Quarantine," or "Deceased".
    - Search & Filter: Locate roosters by ID or breed.

3. Farm Supply & Inventory
Purpose: Tracks physical assets and consumables to prevent shortages and predict future needs.

Features:
    - Inventory Management: Add, update, and delete items in the inventory.
    - Stock Levels: Monitor stock levels and set low stock alerts.
    - Supplier Management: Add and manage suppliers for farm supplies.
    - Purchase Orders: Create and manage purchase orders for supplies.
    - Delivery Tracking: Track the delivery of supplies and update inventory levels.
    - Search & Filter: Locate items by name or category.

4. Sales & Transaction Tracking
Purpose: Records financial transactions and tracks revenue performance.

Features:
    - Sales History: View a list of all sales transactions with details such as Date, Amount, and Payment Method.
    - Revenue Analysis: Generate reports showing total revenue, average sale amount, and top-selling items.
    - Payment Processing: Accept payments through various methods (e.g., cash, credit card, or online payments).
    - Search & Filter: Locate transactions by date, amount, or payment method.

5. Analytics & Reports
Purpose: Visualizes data to help the owner make informed business decisions regarding breeding and sales strategies.

Features:
    - Sales Charts: Visual graphs showing Sales per Staff (Monthly) and Overall Sales (Yearly) with trend lines.
    - Inventory Forecasting: Reports predicting future consumption of feeds and medicine based on historical trends.
    - Health & Production KPIs: Analytics on rooster health and farm productivity.

6. Feedback & Ratings
Purpose: Collects and displays customer sentiment to improve farm reputation and service quality.

Features:
    - Customer Reviews: View a list of all customer reviews with details such as Date, Rating, and Comment.
    - Rating Analysis: Generate reports showing average rating, top-rated items, and customer feedback trends.
    - Search & Filter: Locate reviews by date, rating, or customer name.
